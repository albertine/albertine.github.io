Contest Details


Project information

Gate is an advanced opensource software developed by the international OpenGATE collaboration and dedicated to numerical simulations in medical imaging and radiotherapy. It currently supports simulations of Emission Tomography (Positron Emission Tomography - PET and Single Photon Emission Computed Tomography - SPECT), Computed Tomography (CT), Optical Imaging (Bioluminescence and Fluorescence) and Radiotherapy experiments. Using an easy-to-learn macro mechanism to configurate simple or highly sophisticated experimental settings, GATE now plays a key role in the design of new medical imaging devices, in the optimization of acquisition protocols and in the development and assessment of image reconstruction algorithms and correction techniques. It can also be used for dose calculation in radiotherapy experiments.

Web site :  http://www.opengatecollaboration.org


Who can submit a logo?

The competition is open to anyone, whether professional or amateur, scientist or artist or both.

Deadline

The contest is open for two months, from 29th January 2017 midnight (GMT) until 1st April 2017 midnight (GMT).

Selection of the winner

All entries will be screened and those that comply with the Terms and conditions and have met the guidelines and specifications, as seen on this page, will be judged by the OpenGate collaboration members (~20 people) by a vote.

The winner will be announced on April 1st and will be notified email. In the event that no entry is selected, Gate reserves the right to declare no winner and run the contest again at a later date.

The winning entry will be displayed on this page after that date. The judges decision will be final.
